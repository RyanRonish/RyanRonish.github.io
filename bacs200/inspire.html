Elon Musk is an entreprenuer and innovator who has changed every industry he gets involved with such as the auto industry the space industry, and the payment industry. When he was younger Elon escaped South Africa during apartheid and came to the United States. From there he went to college, dropped out and founded X.com, which would later become Paypal.

After that Elon sold Paypal and got into the electric vehicle industry and founded Tesla. At the same time he helped found SpaceX. These have been very successful ventures for Elon and he has changed the industries forever. He has achieved the feat of becoming the richest man in the world and continues to dream about tomorrow.
