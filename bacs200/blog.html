Research Question

What are the positive impacts of social media that are often overlooked?

Positives

  • Easy way to distribute information
  • Crowdsourcing
  • Access to learning communities

Negatives

  • Spread of Misinformation
  • Polarization and Extremism
  • Addiction

Political implications

It can be hard to find the benefits of social media. One benefit of social media is the distribution of information is far easier with social media platforms. This has leveled the playing field in elections for canidates challenging incumbants. Both Congresswoman Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and former President Donald Trump utilized social media platforms to win elections against established politicians.

Economic implications

Financial markets were disrupted when the WSB traders decided to mount a successful short squeeze on companies they liked. Social media was used as a vessel to democritize trading for individuals who believed the markets were largely unfair and rigged against them.

Social implications

In addition, social media has increased the ease for crowdsourcing. An example of this was when MIT brought 10,000 users to its crowdsourcing platform that gave birth to HEAT which is a energy assessment technology that is helping fight climate change.

Lastly social media has increased the access to learning communities for both privleged and underprivleged students. "Overwhelming evidence on the positive impact of social media for immigrants learning eanglish" (Barrot, 2021). There are many avenues within social media platforms where students can go to expand their educational horizons.
