Research Question

What are the positive impacts of social media that are often overlooked?

Positives

  • Easy way to distribute information
  • Crowdsourcing
  • Access to learning communities

Negatives

  • Spread of Misinformation
  • Polarization and Extremism
  • Addiction

Looking at both the positives and the negatives of social media it is hard to conclude if it is a good or bad thing. With social media being relativly new to most of our society and most of our society having a lack of understanding when it comes to anything related to technology/ computers. It can be hard to find the benefits of social media. One benefit of social media is the distrobution of information is far easier with social media platforms. This has:

  • Helped democritize financial markets
  • Leveled the playing field in elections for canidates challenging incumbants
  • "Overwhelming evidence on the positive impact of social media for language learning" (Barrot, 2021)

In addition, social media has increased the ease for crowdsourcing. An example of this was when MIT brought 10,000 users to its crowdsourcing platform that gave birth to HEAT which is a energy assessment technology that is helping fight climate change.

Lastly social media has increased the access to learning communities for both privleged and underprivleged students. There are many avenues within social media platforms where students can go to expand their educational horizons.
