Research Process

Linear Research Process

  1. Define the topic of your paper
  2. Ask a research question
  3. Find sources that relate to the question
  4. Organize your sources/information from the sources
  5. Formulate themes based off of your sources
  6. Make a referances page for citations
  7. Consolidate the information you've found
  8. Write the research paper or project

Fluid Research Process

  • Find/ define a topic for your paper
  • Workshop a research question (general idea)
  • Find surface level sources for background knowledge about the topic
  • Consolidate sources and information thusfar
  • Start your citations/ references page
  • Begin writting/ working on the project
  • Find specific sources for your question
  • Summon/databases with peer-reviewed scholarly journals
  • Consolidate your sources and information
  • Add to your references page
  • Revisit your question, ensure it's specific
  • Workshop themes and overall concepts
  • Consolidate themes picking the best ones to focus on
  • Continue to work on your project/paper
  • Finish work on project/paper and references page
